Object B: Blue Mug

My second object is a Blue mug. From the Comc store when I go there I go to the Dollarama right after. Mainly because at the dollar store I can find a lot of cheap things to experiment with and occupy my time.

This Mug is important because this is where my business started and how I came to make the decision to study Graphic Design at Niagara College. What happened was is that I was at home and it had been 4 months since I had left my house other then to do grocery shopping so I was a little stir crazy. I went to the dollar the Canadian tire and I saw an engraving pen and thought that I should mess around with it.

I went to the Dollarama and got a bunch of glasses and mugs (including this one) I started carving on them and my cousin saw them and asked me if I could engrave his logo on them and so I did. After setting up an Ets shop and doing a few commissions I thought I should look into studying graphic design and learn to expand my business. One Blue mug set me on a whole new path.
